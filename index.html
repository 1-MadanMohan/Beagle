The Beagle

The Beagle is a breed of small hound that is known for its keen sense of smell and tracking instinct. They are friendly, curious, and great with families. These dogs are often used for hunting and as detection dogs due to their excellent olfactory abilities. Beagles are typically friendly, curious, and great with families. They are known for their playful nature and love for exploration. Beagles are also very social animals, often getting along well with other dogs and pets. Beagles are generally healthy dogs, but they can be prone to certain health issues.
